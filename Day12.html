Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir is a Hindu temple in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is located at the site of Ram Janmabhoomi, the site believed to be the birthplace of Rama, an avatar of Vishnu and principal deity of Hinduism.
